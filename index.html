PrashantAdvait Foundation

Creating a new humanity through intelligent spirituality.

Spirituality is not the renunciation of life; it is the art of living fully.
~Acharya Prashant

PrashantAdvait Foundation is a non-profit socio-spiritual organisation founded and led by Acharya Prashant. It was established with an objective to demolish falseness and promote a scientific as well as spiritual attitude towards life.

The Foundation facilitates Acharya Prashant's discourses at various platforms and organises spiritual retreats, online sessions, lectures, conferences, and one-to-one counselling with him. These discourses are recorded and broadcast worldwide via numerous social media, so that these Teachings, that are at heart of the mission, reach as many in the limited time as possible.

The Foundation works with a missionary zeal towards creating an intelligent, fact-based and wise human society; and the mission functions mainly through a set of devoted volunteers, each being themselves rooted deeply in the teachings of Acharya Prashant.

Over the years more and more volunteers from various fields of life have joined the Foundation, working relentlessly to help spread the Teachings. Today, the Foundation has touched and affected the lives of more than a million individuals, and these numbers are only going to multiply in the coming times.
